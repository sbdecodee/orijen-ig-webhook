Privacy Policy

Orijen RD Messaging is a private internal application used to manage Instagram Business communications for the Orijen RD franchise.

Data Collection

This application processes the following data through Meta Platforms APIs:

  • Instagram messages and comments
  • Public usernames and user IDs
  • Message timestamps and interaction metadata

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Detect predefined keywords in comments and messages
  • Trigger internal notifications (such as email alerts)
  • Support customer service and moderation workflows

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for operational needs and is not stored permanently unless required for audit or support purposes.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data by contacting:

Email: sbernal@decodeegroup.com

Third-Party Services

This application integrates with Meta Platforms, Inc. (Instagram and Messenger APIs).
